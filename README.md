## 🌟 Overview

**Islami** is a premium, modern, and high-performance Flutter application designed to provide a seamless spiritual experience for Muslims. Built with a focus on **Visual Excellence**, it combines essential Islamic tools into a single, beautiful interface.

Whether you're reciting the Holy Quran, keeping track of your daily Azkar, or listening to live spiritual radio, Islami provides a serene and intuitive environment to help you stay connected to your faith.

---

## ✨ Key Capabilities

### 📖 The Holy Quran
Experience the divine word of Allah with a beautifully formatted Quran reader.
-   **Full 114 Surahs**: Access all surahs with clear, readable Arabic typography.
-   **Audio Recitations**: Listen to top reciters within the app.
-   **Last Read Support**: Never lose your place in your spiritual journey.

### 📜 Authenticated Hadith
Explore a rich collection of Prophetic traditions (Ahadith).
-   **Curated Collections**: Access influential Hadith books and narrations.
-   **Visual Presentation**: Modern cards designed for focused reading.

### 📿 Digital Sebha (Tasbeeh)
A modern take on the traditional rosary to help you maintain your daily Dhikr.
-   **Interactive Counter**: Simple, intuitive tap-to-count interface.
-   **Daily Goals**: Stay consistent with your praise and remembrance.
-   **Beautiful Transitions**: Smooth animations that make long sessions effortless.

### 📻 Islamic Radio
Stay inspired on the go with high-quality Islamic radio streams.
-   **Global Stations**: Listen to various Quranic and Islamic FM stations.
-   **Background Play**: Enjoy spiritual content while multitasking.

### 🕋 Prayer Times & Azkar
Stay mindful of your daily obligations with precise timing and supplications.
-   **Accurate Salah Times**: Real-time prayer timings based on location.
-   **Morning & Evening Azkar**: Comprehensive supplications for protection and peace.

---

## 🛠 Modern Tech Stack

Developed with industry-leading standards to ensure scalability and performance:

-   **Frontend**: Flutter (Dart) with a heavy focus on custom, responsive UI.
-   **Architecture**: `MVVM` (Model-View-ViewModel) for clean separation of concerns.
-   **State Management**: `BLoC` for business logic and `Provider` for lightweight state.
-   **Local Storage**: `SharedPreferences` for user settings and progress tracking.
-   **Audio Engine**: `just_audio` for high-fidelity playback.
-   **Design System**: Custom design based on a dark/golden luxury aesthetic.

## 🚀 Getting Started

To get a local copy up and running, follow these simple steps:

### Prerequisites
-   Flutter SDK installed ([Installation Guide](https://docs.flutter.dev/get-started/install))
-   A code editor (VS Code or Android Studio)

### Installation
1.  **Clone the repo**:
    ```bash
    git clone https://github.com/Mustafa-Mohamed26/islami_app.git
    ```
2.  **Fetch dependencies**:
    ```bash
    flutter pub get
    ```
3.  **Run the application**:
    ```bash
    flutter run
    ```
